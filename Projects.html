Escape Paradox

Escape Paradox is the name of my thesis project for my Bachelor's Degree. I designed concept art and a basic animation
for a retro style surrealist horror game. It is a project I hope to finish in my spare time as I put a lot of planning and
thought into it, and really enjoyed the process of creating a FMV game single-handedly from scratch.

Astroturf (Gamejam 2022)

Astroturf was a game created for GameJam 2022. It was inspired by Stardew Valley and Paper Mario. You are an astronaut
in space, terroforming asteroids in order to grow space plants for money so that you can afford upgrades for your spaceship.
The player only has 30 days until the blackhole destroys their asteroids so the player must finish upgrading their spaceship
before time is up. You can play the full game here!

Spellbinder (Kiwijam 2022)

Spellbinder was a game created for Kiwijam 2022. In this game you are a wizard who runs a potion shop. Customers order
potions and you must mix the right ingredients in the correct order to successfully make their potion. You can play the full game here!
