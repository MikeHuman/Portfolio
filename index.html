About Me

Hi! I’m Mikey, digital artist, motion designer, and web designer. My primary focus is on 2D animation, pixel art, and game design,
but I am a keen and passionate learner in almost any area of art. I have a Bachelor’s in Motion Design and have a wide variety of
knowledge in video editing and motion graphics. I also have a wide variety of skills in animation, game design, and digital art.
Over the summer holidays of my Bachelor’s Degree, I completed a Diploma of Web Design and Development from Open Polytechnic.

I have always had an interest in programming and being able to combine those skills with my artistic skills has created many
opportunities for me. I have proficiency in Photoshop, After Effects, Premiere Pro, Illustrator, Clip Studio Paint, Paint Tool SAI, HTML, CSS,
JavaScript, PHP and SQL. I am fresh out of university and ready to start working in any artistic area!


You can find me on Twitter, ArtStation, or LinkedIn.
